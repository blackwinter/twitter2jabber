    twitter2jabber - Twitter-to-Jabber gateway.¶ ↑

    VERSION¶ ↑

    This documentation refers to twitter2jabber version 0.8.2

    DESCRIPTION¶ ↑

    Allows you to read Twitter streams with your favourite Jabber client.

    Based on a script by dkam. See also his Jitter project.
